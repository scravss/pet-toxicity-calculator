Emergency Care Guides

First Steps After Toxin Ingestion
Stay calm, identify the substance and estimated amount, then call your vet or a poison helpline immediately. Do not induce vomiting unless specifically instructed by a professional.
Understanding Veterinary ABCs
Emergency vets assess Airway, Breathing, and Circulation first. Bringing the substance packaging and knowing your pet's weight can significantly speed up diagnosis and treatment.
