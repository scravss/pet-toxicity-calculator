The science behind the numbers

🔬
Resting Energy Requirement (RER) is the baseline calories needed for basic body functions at rest. Calculated as: RER = 70 × (body weight in kg)0.75
⚡
Maintenance Energy Requirement (MER) = RER × a lifestyle multiplier that accounts for activity, reproductive status, and life stage. This is the number you should actually feed.
